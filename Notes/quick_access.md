# ⚡ Quick Access Toolbar

![Quick Access Screenshot](../assets/quick_access.png)

> The bar just beneath the main menu gives one‑click access to the features you use most. Each icon has a tooltip and keyboard shortcut for maximum convenience.

---

## 🔹 Buttons & descriptions

- **+ New** ➕ – Start a brand‑new recipe from scratch. Opens the add‑recipe dialog where you can enter name, ingredients, directions and more.

- **Collections** 📁 – Manage your recipe collections. Use this to create, view and organize custom groups like "Dinner Favorites" or "Holiday Baking." Click a collection to filter the main list.

- **Shopping** 🛒 – Show or hide the shopping list drawer. Add ingredients directly from recipes, check items off as you shop, and export or copy the list.

- **Pantry** 🥫 – Open the pantry inventory. Track what you have on hand and get “Can Make Now” suggestions. Items persist across sessions.

- **Suggest** 💡 – Ask the app for recipe suggestions based on pantry contents or dietary preferences. Great when you’re not sure what to cook.

- **Notes** 🗒️ – Access your saved notes. Add personal thoughts to recipes, jot down modifications, or keep general cooking tips.

- **Gallery** 🖼️ – Launch the photo gallery showing all uploaded recipe images. A visual way to browse your collection; click any image to open its recipe.

## 💡 Usage tips

- Hover over an icon to see its name and shortcut (e.g. `Ctrl/Cmd + X` for new recipe).
- The toolbar is responsive – icons condense on narrow screens and remain accessible via dropdown on mobile.
- You can toggle the entire quick access bar in **Settings** under "Interface" if you prefer a minimalist view.

## 🛠 Notes for users

- The toolbar reflects your current context; some icons may appear disabled when not applicable (e.g. Collections when you have none).
- You can reorder toolbar items in settings by dragging their labels. Changes save automatically.
- The icons use FontAwesome; if you install the icon plugin in Obsidian, they’ll render inline here.

Use the Quick Access toolbar to glide through the app – it’s designed to keep your workflow smooth and your hands on the keyboard or mouse, whichever you like.
