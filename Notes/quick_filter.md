# 🔍 Quick Filters & Advanced Filtering

> Use these controls to narrow your recipe list instantly. The filters sit at the top of the main view and adapt to your selections – no need to remember menus or search syntax.

---

## 🧁 Quick Filters

Quick filters give you one‑click access to commonly used categories and courses.

- They appear as brightly colored buttons (e.g. Smoothies, Appetizers, Soups/Stews) and update as you add or remove recipes.
- Click a button to immediately show only recipes matching that filter; click again to remove it.
- Multiple quick filters can be combined – the list updates dynamically.

**When to use:**

- You know the general type of dish you want (e.g. "I feel like something for breakfast").
- You want a fast way to limit results without opening the full filter panel.

## ⚙️ General Filters

The row of dropdowns above Quick Filters gives you fine‑grained control.

- **A‑Z / Rating / Time** – sort the list.
- **All Courses / All Cuisines / Any Time** – choose from available values.
- **Can Make Now** checkbox shows recipes you can prepare with current pantry items.
- Use the `More Filters` button to expand the Advanced panel (see below).

### 🎯 Tips

- Start by selecting a course or cuisine to reduce the dropdown options, then refine with other filters.
- Type text into any dropdown to jump to matching entries.
- Clearing the `Can Make Now` box and clicking the green "All" button resets everything.

## 🧪 Advanced Filters

Click **More Filters** or the sliders icon to open the expanded panel (screenshot below).

- **Tags / Sub‑Categories / Dietary** – filter by user‑defined tags, more specific categories, or dietary types like vegan and gluten‑free.
- **Ratings** – show recipes with a given star rating.
- Favorites toggle ⭐ lets you focus on recipes you’ve marked as favorites.
- The panel can be collapsed by clicking **More Filters** again or pressing `Esc`.

**Why it’s powerful:**

- Mix multiple criteria to drill down to exactly what you need.
- Filters persist across sessions until you clear them, so your next visit starts where you left off.

## 📝 User notes

- On mobile, the filter panel slides up from the bottom for easier thumb reach.
- Filtering happens instantly – there’s no "Apply" button.
- You can save particularly useful combinations as collections via the collections menu.

Use these tools to quickly find the recipe you want, even in a library of hundreds. Happy cooking!
